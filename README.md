# Shopping-Behavior-Product-Ranking-Analysis-Power-BI-Dashboard

This project analyzes the shopping behavior of 3,900 customers, focusing on purchase frequency, product preferences, payment methods, seasonality, and review data. The dashboard was built in Power BI to help visualize customer trends and support retail decision-making.

## Project Objective

To explore customer purchasing patterns and product performance across multiple categories to help businesses understand buying behavior, improve product strategy, and optimize marketing and inventory decisions.

## Key Questions / KPIs

Which product categories are purchased the most?

What is the average customer age?

What payment methods do shoppers prefer?

How often do customers make purchases?

Which seasons drive the most demand for each category?

What size preferences exist across product types?

How do customers rate products by category?

What percentage of customers use discounts?

## Project Insights

Clothing is the most purchased category across all demographics.

Average customer age is 44, suggesting a mature shopper base.

Credit card and PayPal are the most popular payment methods.

Customers most commonly shop monthly or quarterly, not weekly.

Fall and spring are the strongest shopping seasons for most categories.

Size preference varies by category, but Medium and Large are the most common.

Review ratings show most products fall between 3 and 5 stars, indicating fairly positive satisfaction.

## Final Conclusion

The analysis shows that clothing dominates sales volume, with most customers shopping seasonally and preferring digital payment methods. Discount use is common but not universal. Businesses can leverage these insights to improve stock planning, seasonal promotions, and customer targeting strategies.
